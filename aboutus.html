ABOUT US

Malaysia's ultimate IOFC Centre, where nature & technology co-exist to provide an environment for emerging offshore activities, is a town of opportunities and challenges. In order to facilitate the development of Wilayah Persekutuan Labuan as an International Offshore Financial Centre, the Financial Park Complex was established. Financial Park, standing majestically by the Wilayah Persekutuan Labuan town centre, embodies the true essence of modern luxury and convenience. It is advantageously situated very near to the airport and within a walking distance to the sea front. Financial Park offers you more than property investment; it offers you an international market with an edge in quality, prestige and great growth potential. It is a centre of business far away from the hustle and bustle of the city where BUSINESS & LEISURE mix and mix effectively.

SHOP LOTS

It is a strenuous process to find the right affordable shop space and location for their business. Financial Park Complex is poised to become an exceptional centre of business and leisure by offering two storey of shop unit with spaces ranging from 205 square feet to 968 square feet as well as ample parking space providing a wide range of layouts to suit any business and requirements, from department stores and supermarkets to restaurant and duty free shops are available in the complex.

FACILITIES

The Financial Park Convention Hall

The Financial Park Convention Hall is Labuan’s most technologically advanced and purpose built multipurpose hall or performing arts centre that can accommodate up to 1,700 people for theatre style and up to 1,000 diners. There are several varying sizes of executive meeting or seminar rooms which can accommodate between 10 to 300 people giving more venue options to event planners and organisers. The accessibility and availability of halls of various sizes and types fit a wide range of events at Financial Park Complex and the comprehensive facilities enable meetings, incentives, conferences and exhibitions (MICE) to be run smoothly.
